Privacy Policy for Lagisht

Last Updated: 20/02/2025

Thank you for using Lagisht (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your financial data remains completely under your control. Lagisht is designed as a fully offline application, meaning that no personal or usage data is collected, stored, or transmitted over the internet.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

  • Personal Information: We do not require or collect your phone number, email address, or any other personal details.
  • Expense Data: All expense records remain solely on your device. No information is transmitted to external servers.
  • Usage Data: We do not track your interactions with the App.
  • Device Information: We do not collect any device-specific data such as device model or operating system.

2. Data Processing and Storage

All data entered into Lagisht is processed locally on your device. There is no cloud backup or remote storage involved. This approach ensures that your sensitive financial information remains private and secure.
